# 🧬 [About MedrixFlow 2.0](https://github.com/bytedance/medrix-flow)

> **From Open Source, Back to Open Source**

**MedrixFlow** (**Med**ical **R**esearch **I**ntelligence and Data-driven e**X**ploration **Flow**) is a community-driven SuperAgent harness that researches, codes, and creates.
With the help of sandboxes, memories, tools and skills, it handles
different levels of tasks that could take minutes to hours.
